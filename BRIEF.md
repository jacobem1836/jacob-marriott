## Voice & Tone

**Adjectives:** warm · direct · curious · adventurous · grounded

**Register:** conversational but professional. Reads like someone talking, not a corporate bio.

### Signature moves (from his own writing — mimic these)
- **Em dashes** — used constantly to insert colour mid-sentence
- **Parenthetical asides with personality** — e.g. *"(pretty sweet gig, thanks Mum and Dad!)"*, *"(while also playing bass in the jazz bands I was running the tech for)"*
- **Narrative over bullet points** — connects disparate experiences into a thread
- **Enthusiasm without boasting** — *"completely hooked"*, *"loved every moment"*, *"itching for new experiences"*
- **Confident but accessible** — states what he brings, no puffery
- **First person throughout**

### Avoid
- Stiff corporate-speak
- Safe, generic portfolio copy
- Fake humility or over-claiming
- Tech-bro buzzword density
